Rushees

Zeta Beta Tau at the University of Washington is more than just a fraternity, it's a family. Brotherhood is the most important aspect of our house, and what sets us apart is the diversity of the brothers. Our family hails from all over, including many students from outside the country, including Thailand, Turkey, Ireland and more! Our differences are what provide unique points-of-view and experiences for all to cultivate. The connections within our brotherhood are truly what makes this house a home, so come see for yourself and meet some of the fine gentlemen of Zeta Beta Tau!
